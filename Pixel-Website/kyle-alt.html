God of War

God of War follows Kratos and his son, Atreus, as they traverse the nine realms of Norse mythology on a quest to spread his deceased wife's ashes. Along the way, they come into conflict with Thor, Odin and other characters from the Norse mythos. The game received multiple awards upon release in 2018, and is considered by critics to be one of the greatest games of all time. Red Dead Redemption 2

Set in 1899 during the decline of the Wild West, players step into the boots of Arthur Morgan as he runs from the law with his gang of outlaws. Forced to deal with the loss of the only lifestyle he has ever known and the collapse of the gang, Red Dead Redemption 2 is considered by many to be one of the best single player games ever made.
